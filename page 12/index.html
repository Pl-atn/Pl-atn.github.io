2nd album: Replica

Vaundy's second full-length album, replica, released in late 2023, is an ambitious and sprawling project that reflects both his artistic growth and his desire to push the boundaries of J-pop.

Total Tracks: 35 songs across two discs.

  • Disc 1:

    Features new material and "replica versions" of earlier hits like "Kaiju no Hanauta" and "Backlight."

  • Disc 2:

    A chronological compilation of singles released over the past three years.

Vaundy described the album as his "graduation project", marking the end of his university life and a new chapter in his music career

Themes and Sound

  • Conceptual Depth:

    The title replica reflects Vaundy's exploration of originality and imitation in art. He likens music to geological layers—each song a product of accumulated influences.

  • Sound Design:

    Disc 1 includes interludes like "Audio 006" and "Audio 007," which create a cinematic, immersive experience. The sound is more cohesive than Strobo, with a stronger rock influence and experimental touches.

  • Genre Fusion:

    Still rooted in J-pop and alt-rock, but with more British rock and ambient influences.

Brief about the album: "replica" is the second album by Vaundy, released on November 15, 2023. It is a 2-disc set containing a total of 35 songs. Disc 1 contains new songs including "replica", "ZERO", and "NEO JAPAN", while Disc 2 is a compilation of previously released hit songs such as "Chainsaw Blood", "Todome's Blows", and "Naked Hero".

The album achieved significant success, debuting at #2 on the Oricon Weekly Albums Chart and Billboard Japan Hot Albums. It also topped the Oricon Weekly Digital Albums Chart for two consecutive weeks.
